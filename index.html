Frequently Asked Questions

1 Do you bring your own cleaning supplies and equipment?
Yes, we bring all necessary supplies and equipment. However, if you have specific products or equipment you’d like us to use, we’re happy to accommodate.
2 Are your cleaning products safe for children and pets?
Yes, we use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for children, pets, and those with sensitivities. We’re committed to providing a clean, healthy environment for your home or workplace.
3 Do I need to be home during the cleaning service?
No, you do not need to be present. Many clients provide us with access instructions, allowing us to complete the cleaning efficiently while you’re away. We prioritize trust, security, and professionalism.
4 Do you offer one-time cleanings, or only recurring services?
We offer both! Whether you need a one-time deep clean or recurring services on a weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly basis, we can accommodate your needs.
5 How do you determine pricing for your services?
Our prices are based on the size of the area, type of cleaning, and specific requirements. We offer free quotes so you can understand costs upfront, with no hidden fees.
6 In what areas are your services available?
We focus our services in Newcastle upon Tyne and surrounding areas, such as Blaydon, Gateshead, Whickham and many more!
